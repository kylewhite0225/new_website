ABOUT KYLE

MANUFACTURING & MECHANICAL ENGINEER

I have 4 years of experience as a manufacturing engineer in the aerospace industry, one year with Hexcel Composites and three with AvtechTyee Aerospace.

At Hexcel, I focused on 5-axis CNC machining of honeycomb composite structures and their associated tooling design. My role at AvtechTyee as a manufacturing engineer involved the process design, process planning, CNC machine programming, new product introduction, and continuing production engineering support of structural aircraft components.

While attending WWU, I was involved as the lead suspension engineer and CNC machinist for WWU Racing's Viking 58. My suspension designs were successfully utilized for three generations of competition vehicles, and I graduated from Western honored as the manufacturing engineering department's outstanding graduate with cumulative GPA of 3.7.

COMPUTER SCIENCE STUDENT

In April of 2020, the pandemic brought an unexpected change to my life (unemployment) which allowed me to act on career priorities that I had been re-evaluating for some time. My goal is to transition my career into environmental restoration using engineering and technology in order to use my skills for the mitigation of climate change.

In June of 2020, I applied for the B.S. program in computer science at Bellevue College and immediately began taking courses in Java, C++, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and database systems. My projected graduation date is June 2022 and my current GPA is 3.97.
